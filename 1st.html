Author Name - Rhonda Byrne

The Secret by Rhonda Byrne was published on November 28, 2006

Short descriptions or summaries

The Secret is a 2006 self-help book by Rhonda Byrne that's based on the pseudoscientific law of attraction. The book explores the idea that our thoughts and feelings determine what we attract in life, and how to use that power to create a better, happier life

Short descriptions or summaries

Ikigai: The Japanese Secret to a Long and Happy Life by Hector Garcia Puigcerver and Francesc Miralles is a 2016 book that explores the Japanese concept of finding purpose in life.
